Welcome to Relay Node - your gateway to the distributed ASCII cinema network.

## Mission Brief

This network serves as a hub for ASCII art streaming, telnet cinema, and text-based entertainment distribution. Connect to our nodes via SSH or Telnet to experience classic films, animations, and live art performances rendered in pure text.

## Quick Connect

```
ssh viewer@relay.example.com -p 2222
```

Browse our transmission archive or check the current streaming schedule.
